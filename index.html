SIRS Criteria

0/4
No SIRS criteria met — continue routine assessment.

If ≥2 SIRS → Sepsis Bundle

  • Get lactate. If lactate ≥2 or suspicion high → initiate sepsis bundle.
  • Blood cultures before antibiotics.
  • Broad-spectrum antibiotics within 1 hour.
  • 30 mL/kg crystalloid for hypotension or lactate ≥4.
  • Reassess perfusion & repeat lactate.
